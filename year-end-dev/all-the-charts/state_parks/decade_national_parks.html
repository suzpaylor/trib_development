Utah national park visitors

No where is the recreation boom on Utah's public lands more apparent than at its national parks, especially Zion, one of the nation's crown jewels. Over the past decade, visits increased by nearly 73 percent to more than 15 million at the "Mighty 5" and eight others destinations administered by the National Park Service.
